About Polish & Plait Beauty Bar

Polish & Plait Beauty Bar started in a bedroom as an idea where a space would be created for women to feel warmth, peace, inclusivity, and love. It would be a space that offers women and everyone who comes through its doors silence while getting their services done if that is what they need, an ear when they need someone to just listen to them, and a therapeutic environment to just feel carefree.

We offer a wide range of services including manicures, pedicures, lash extensions, waxing services, and cornrows. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing you with the best beauty experience.
